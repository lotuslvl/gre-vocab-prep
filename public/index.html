Ace the GRE with Your Friends!

Getting ready for the GRE can be tough. Prep books line your bookshelves ,but it is still difficult to muster up the energy to study.

Prepi gets you ready for your big day by pairing traditional study prep with friendly,gamified competition.

It's simple. Use our practice tests to get you ready and monitor your progress.

When you are ready, pair yourself with a study buddy and set practice jam sessions where you can answer questions head-to-head.
