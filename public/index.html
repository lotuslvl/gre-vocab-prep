Ace the Test with Your Friends!

Getting ready for the exams can be tough. Prep books line your bookshelves ,but it is still difficult to muster up the energy to study.

Prepi provides a simple platform to take practice tests and view your progresss overtime.

It's simple. Use our practice tests to get you ready with hints and unlimited time.

When you are ready, pair yourself with a study buddy and set practice jam sessions where you can answer questions head-to-head.


Technologies Used:

HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, mySQL, Socket.io, Wordnik API, Bootstrap, Sequelize, Express
